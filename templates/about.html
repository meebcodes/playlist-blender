PlaylistBlender

PlaylistBlender is a tool that uses the Spotify API to generate appealing gradient images from public Spotify playlists. These images will reflect the mood and feel of the playlist, and are even formatted so you can upload them as cover art!

To get started, copy the URL of a public Spotify playlist and enter it into the form. Then press "Get blends" to get your results!

About & How-To

PlaylistBlender is a web app that allows you to generate gradient images that reflect the mood and feel of your Spotify playlists.

These images are generated from an algorithm that takes into account the averaged musical attributes of songs on your playlist and outputs an image with colors chosen based on those averages. For example, a playlist with lots of cheery, upbeat songs might generate a bright, highly-saturated image. A playlist with lots of chill piano tracks, on the other hand, might produce something more muted and soft.

All of these musical attributes are pulled from the Spotify API. PlaylistBlender doesn't need (or have) access to any part of your Spotify account, so don't worry--this app won't make any kinds of changes to the playlists you submit. (This is also why PlaylistBlender is currently limited to public playlists!)

To copy a link to a playlist, first navigate to that playlist in your mobile or desktop Spotify app. Then:

Mobile

Click the three dots at the top of the playlist. Then click Share > Copy link.

Desktop

Click the three dots at the top of the playlist. Then hover over Share and click Copy link to playlist.
